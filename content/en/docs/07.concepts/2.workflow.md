Worcable’s workflow is designed to transform the recruitment process into a smooth, collaborative, and fully controlled experience.

## Core Concepts

Worcable revolves around transforming raw data (the CV) into a concrete recruitment step (the candidate in the pipeline).

* **Centralization**: A single entry point for all your applications.  
* **Collaboration**: Real-time sharing of feedback and scores between recruiters and managers.  
* **Sovereignty**: Full control over your process and your data.  

## The Customizable Pipeline

The operational core of Worcable is its visual pipeline. Unlike rigid solutions, Worcable adapts to your internal methodology.

### Step Flexibility
Every organization has its own processes. Worcable allows you to:
* **Create your own columns** (e.g., *Technical Test*, *Culture Fit Interview*, *Executive Approval*).  
* **Reorganize the workflow** depending on the job type (SaaS, HR, Marketing).  
* **Automate statuses** with simple drag-and-drop interactions.

## Automation & Productivity

Thanks to its modular architecture, Worcable simplifies tracking:
* **Full history**: Every candidate movement is recorded.  
* **Smart filters**: Find talent by extracted skills or custom tags.  
* **Native or Docker mode**: Access your tools regardless of your hosting environment.  

## Workflow Philosophy

Worcable is designed to make recruitment more **human** by automating the **technical** aspects. The goal is to reduce time spent on administration so you can focus on what truly matters: connecting with your future team members.
