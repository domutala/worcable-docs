Worcable is not just another ATS (*Applicant Tracking System*). It is a **sovereign recruitment infrastructure**, designed to break down the silos of proprietary recruiting solutions and give engineers and recruiters full control over their talent pipeline.

## The Problem: ATS Opacity
Most current recruiting platforms suffer from four major issues:
* **Vendor Lock-in**: Your candidate data is trapped in closed databases.
* **Structural Rigidity**: Predefined workflows that never adapt to the agility of a startup.
* **Linear Costs**: Per-seat or per-job posting pricing that penalizes growth.
* **Black Box**: No visibility into how resumes are parsed or scored.

## The Worcable Vision: "Recruitment as Code"

Worcable is built on a simple belief: **Recruitment is a critical engine of any company, and that engine should be open source.**

### 1. Decoupled Architecture & High Performance
Worcable is built on a modern **Nuxt 3 / Vue 3** stack leveraging hybrid rendering (SSR/ISR) for optimal SEO performance (Google Jobs). The architecture follows a **Modular Monolith** approach, allowing heavy components (like AI-powered parsing) to be isolated while maintaining a smooth DX (*Developer Experience*).

### 2. Sovereignty & Self-Hosting
Deployment is **Docker-first**. Whether you're running on a low-cost VPS or a Kubernetes cluster, Worcable adapts. You own your database, storage volumes, and history. **Your candidates, your data, your infrastructure.**

### 3. Intelligent Parsing (AI & NLP)
Unlike basic solutions, Worcable delegates data extraction to a dedicated service (`cvparser`) built with **Python and FastAPI**. This enables integration of advanced NLP models to structure unstructured data (PDF, DOCX) without impacting UI responsiveness.
